<b>Carry your Dobbscoin in your pocket!</b> Send and receive DOBB instantly by scanning QR codes. A secure, self-custody wallet that gives you full control of your coins. Based on the battle-tested Bitcoin Wallet codebase.


<b>FEATURES</b>

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is decentralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Dobbscoin amount in DOBB, mDOBB and µDOBB.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Dobbscoin via NFC, QR codes or Dobbscoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Dobbscoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
• App widget for Dobbscoin balance.


<b>ABOUT DOBBSCOIN</b>

Dobbscoin (DOBB) is a fun cryptocurrency inspired by the Church of the SubGenius. Join the Slack revolution!
